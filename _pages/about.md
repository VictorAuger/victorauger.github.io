Hello,

I am a Ph.D. candidate in social-cognitive psychology under the supervision of Prof. Alice Normand and Prof. Céline Darnon. My work focuses on the consequences of poverty and economic inequality. Specifically I am interested in the cognitive modifications associated a lacking economic resources in contexts marked by various levels of inequality.

I also teach at Université Clermont Auvergne (UCA), France.
